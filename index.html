About Me

I’m Sohail Ansari, also known as Saheb Ali, a BSc Biology student from Sekhwania Khurd, Kushinagar, Uttar Pradesh, set to graduate in 2026. Hailing from a small village, I turned my passion for education into action by founding EduBack, a self-designed educational platform on Blogger, where I create and upload original study materials and books for students and competitive exam aspirants. My entrepreneurial spirit led me to excel in affiliate marketing, becoming a top earner at EarnKaro and earning an invitation to EarnKaro Affluence 2023, while promoting giants like Unacademy, Amazon, and Prepladder. I also manage multiple Telegram channels with over 100,000+ members combined, sharing self-made content, and maintain a strong presence on Instagram, YouTube, Pinterest, and Twitter/X. Currently, I’m preparing for an MBA to scale my impact in education and digital innovation.
